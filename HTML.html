HIYA. MY NAME IS TIA PICKENS. I’M A GRAPHIC AND UX/UI DESIGNER.
Hi! My name is Tia Pickens and I am a Graphic and UX/UI Designer. I specialize in creating user-centered experiences that are intuitive, visually appealing and functional, while also solving user problems and translating them into design solutions that enhance usability and satisfaction. 
When it comes to my design thinking and thought process, I start my foundation from using the Double Diamond method, an iterative process in which I often revisit phases of my design journey as I gather more insight and then continuously refine my understanding of the problem until I can deliver a solid final product.
I love seeing the excitement and gratification from users that interact with my craft; "Do what you love, and you'll never work another day in your life" truly does apply to me. I’m a  master of many hats, and work best in any kind of environment that will allow me to use all of my skills to the fullest!
